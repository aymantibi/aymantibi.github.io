About Me

Hello! I am a PhD student in Economics at the University of Mannheim. My research interests include fiscal policy, labor markets, and economic growth. I am passionate about understanding how trade and industrial policies shape economic outcomes.

Research

Current Project: "The Effects of Place-Based Trade Policies on Employment and Exports: Evidence from Egypt's Qualifying Industrial Zones."

I use firm-level customs and labor data to study how geographically targeted trade agreements impact local labor markets and firm behavior.
